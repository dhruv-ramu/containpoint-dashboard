# ContainPoint product context

## Purpose

ContainPoint is compliance software for facilities subject to the SPCC rule. It helps organizations maintain a structured system of record linking the SPCC Plan to assets, inspections, corrective actions, training, incidents, and exports.

## Facility data

Users record oil storage assets, secondary containment units, applicability and qualification assessments, and plan versions. Scheduled inspections can be executed with checklist results and signatures.

## Exports

The application can generate PDF summaries and audit packs from current facility data for internal review or third-party review. Generated documents should be reviewed by qualified personnel; the software does not replace legal or engineering judgment.

## Disclaimer

Answers in the compliance assistant are informational. Users must verify obligations against the current rule, their approved plan, and advice from qualified environmental or legal counsel.
