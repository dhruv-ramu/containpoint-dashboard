# Inspections and training under SPCC

## Inspection program

SPCC Plans must include a schedule and procedures for inspecting oil-related equipment, containment, and transfer areas. Inspection frequency and content should match the facility’s equipment and risks as described in the plan.

## Integrity testing

Some containers and piping may require integrity testing or equivalent measures as specified in the rule and the facility’s plan.

## Personnel training

Oil-handling personnel must receive training in operation and maintenance of equipment, discharge procedures, and applicable sections of the plan. Training must be conducted at least once a year and documented.

## Recordkeeping

Inspection records, training records, and plan amendments support demonstration of implementation during agency inspections or audits.
